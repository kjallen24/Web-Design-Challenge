Latitude vs the Elements

On the site, I will display they ways that latitude directly coordinates to weather patterns. Specifically, this analysis explores how weather changes the closer you get to the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we extracted data from over 500 cities using the OpenWeatherMap API.

 We've provided plots to show the correlation between the latitude and various determining factors, such as: Temperature, Cloudiness, WindSpeed, and Humidity
